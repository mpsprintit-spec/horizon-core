# Abbreviations Module

## Overview

The abbreviations module documents Indonesian abbreviations, acronyms, initialisms, shortened forms, and ambiguity notes. The existing directory name is `abreviations`; keep it unchanged for repository compatibility.

## Representative entries

| Form | Expansion | Type | Register/domain | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `dll.` | `dan lain-lain` | abbreviation | neutral writing | Equivalent to “etc.” |
| `dsb.` | `dan sebagainya` | abbreviation | neutral writing | Similar to `dll.`; often for “and so forth.” |
| `dst.` | `dan seterusnya` | abbreviation | neutral writing | Means “and onward/and so on.” |
| `yg` | `yang` | shortening | informal writing | Avoid in formal documentation. |
| `dgn` | `dengan` | shortening | informal writing | Common in notes or messages. |
| `tdk` | `tidak` | shortening | informal/space-limited | Use full form in formal text. |
| `RI` | `Republik Indonesia` | initialism | governmental | May appear in institutional names. |
| `KTP` | `Kartu Tanda Penduduk` | acronym/initialism | administrative | Indonesian identity card. |
| `SD` | `Sekolah Dasar` | initialism | education | Elementary school. |
| `SMA` | `Sekolah Menengah Atas` | initialism | education | Senior high school. |
| `RS` | `rumah sakit` | initialism | health | Hospital; may appear in names. |
| `BPJS` | `Badan Penyelenggara Jaminan Sosial` | acronym/initialism | public service | Indonesian social security agency context. |

## Ambiguity guidance

Some abbreviations are domain-sensitive. `RS` normally means `rumah sakit` in health contexts, but entries should record domain and usage rather than assuming a single universal meaning.
